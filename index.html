Welcome to San Sebastian!

Welcome to San Sebastián! San Sebastián is a stunning coastal city in the north of Spain, located in the heart of the Basque Country.
Known for its breathtaking La Concha Bay, world-renowned gastronomy, and vibrant cultural festivals, San Sebastián is a must-visit destination.
It's famous for being the city with the highest concentration of Michelin stars per square meter, as well as hosting the prestigious San Sebastián International Film Festival.

SALTY

- Pintxos: Small bites bursting with flavor, a must-try in the Old Town.
- Chipirones en su tinta: Squid cooked in its own ink, served with a rich black sauce.
- Gilda: Pintxo with olive, chili, and anchovy.
- Grilled steak (Txuleta): Juicy Basque-style steak with an unmatched flavor.

SWEET

- Pantxineta: A traditional Basque dessert made of puff pastry, filled with custard, and topped with almonds.
- Goxua: A layered dessert with whipped cream, sponge cake soaked in liquor, custard, and caramelized sugar.
- Cigarrillos de Tolosa: Thin, crispy cookies rolled into a cigar shape, often served with tea or coffee.
- Basque Cheesecake (Tarta de queso): A creamy and slightly burnt cheesecake that has become internationally famous, especially from "La Viña" restaurant.

02Places to visit

"La Concha" Beach

One of the most iconic beaches in Europe. Its perfect bay shape, golden sands, and calm waters make it ideal for strolling,
swimming, or simply enjoying the views.

02Places to visit

"Monte Igueldo"

Offers the best panoramic views of the city and La Concha Bay. There’s also a small vintage amusement park that adds a unique charm.

02Places to visit

Old Town (Parte Vieja)

The historical heart of Donostia. Wander through its narrow streets and enjoy the famous pintxos in local bars. Don’t miss the pintxos

02Places to visit

The Kursaal

This modern building, designed by Rafael Moneo, hosts concerts, exhibitions, and events such as the San Sebastián Film Festival. It’s also located near Zurriola Beach, popular among surfers.

"Tamborrada"

A launch vehicle or carrier rocket is a rocket-propelled
vehicle used to carry a payload from Earth's surface to
space, usually to Earth orbit or beyond. Our WEB-X
carrier rocket is the most powerful in operation. Standing
150 metres tall, it's quite an awe-inspiring sight on the
launch pad!

Santo Tomás

A spaceport or cosmodrome is a site for launching (or
receiving) spacecraft, by analogy to the seaport for ships
or airport for aircraft. Based in the famous Cape
Canaveral, our spaceport is ideally situated to take
advantage of the Earth’s rotation for launch.

SPACE CAPSULE

A space capsule is an often-crewed spacecraft that uses
a blunt-body reentry capsule to reenter the Earth's
atmosphere without wings. Our capsule is where you'll
spend your time during the flight. It includes a space
gym, cinema, and plenty of other activities to keep you
entertained.
